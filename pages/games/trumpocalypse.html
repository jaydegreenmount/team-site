ABOUT TRUMPOCALYPSE

In a world overrun with Trump clones the player has taken it upon themselves to shut down the nearby cloning facility that’s producing the horrific mutants.

Armed with your trusty baseball bat, “Trumpa-Thumpa”, you must gather the canisters of “Truth Serum” required to shut down and destroy the facility. Yes, you can finally beat Trump (clones) to death with a baseball bat just like you’ve always dreamed.
